### 1. User Description

Students who struggle with planning their study schedules, managing multiple subjects, and balancing workloads across weeks. These users want a simple tool that automatically organizes their topics, computes study hours, and builds a revision-friendly plan.

### 2. Pain Points

-Difficulty managing time across different subjects

-Forgetting to include revision for difficult topics

-No structured workflow for planning studies week by week

-Manually creating timetables is time-consuming and confusing

-Not knowing how to balance difficulty, time availability, and priority

### 3. Why This Matters

A well-organized study plan leads to:

-Better time management

-Reduced academic stress

-Higher productivity

-More effective learning and retention

-Clear visibility of daily/weekly commitments

Students often lose marks not due to lack of intelligence, but due to poor planning.
This app solves that.

### 4. MVP Goal

Build an AI-driven study planner that:

-Takes topics + difficulty

-Takes available hours weekly

-Generates a personalized study schedule

-Adds extra revision sessions for difficult topics

-Exports the plan to CSV

-Summarizes the weekly plan and learning goals

This MVP focuses on automation + personalization, not complex AI models.
